Muslim Marriage


The legal contract between a bride and bridegroom as part of an Islamic marriage; the contract of Islamic marriage; Islamic marriage in general.
Nikah is an Arabic term used for marriage. It means “contract”. The Quran specifically refers to marriage as “maithaqun Ghalithun”. This means “a strong agreement”.
The validity of the contract of marriage depends upon these features; in absence of any of these features the marriage is either voidable or void;
  • Consent of parties;
  • Ijab;
  • Qbool
  • Dower ;
  • Competency of parties.

There are certain other essentials which do not render the marriage invalid but directory to be followed;
  • Presence of witnesses;
  • Written form;
  • Registration of marriage;
  • Ceremonies;
  • Promise to marry (engagement)
  • At one meeting;
  • Khutabba-e-Nikkah;
